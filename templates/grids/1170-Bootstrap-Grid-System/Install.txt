

-----x---- INSTALL 1170 BOOTSTRAP GRID SYSYTEM PHOTOSHOP ACTION ----x-----

1. Close Photoshop (if opened)
2. Copy "1170 BS Grid.atn" to C:\Users\__USER__\AppData\Roaming\Adobe\Adobe Photoshop CS5\Presets\Actions
   or Copy "1170 BS Grid.atn" to C:\Program Files\Adobe\Adobe Photoshop CS5\Presets\Actions
3. Restart Photoshop
4. Go to Menu => Window -> Actions or you can press Alt + F9
5. Click on the Action Menu (Right top of the Action panel)
6. Then Click "Load Actions" and Select "1170 BS Grid.atn" from Browse option
7. Now You will see New Group Layer called "1170 BS Grid", Expand it and click "12 Col"
8. Click Play on the toolbar and Wait until the action complete
9. Now you can see a new document with 1170px Grid System
10. Yay! You are done.
